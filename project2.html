HYGGE

A SMALL SCALE VR INSTALLATION

  • MEDIUM:


    TILTBRUSH

  • TIMEFRAME:


    NOVEMBER 2021

  • SETTING:


    PARSONS SCHOOL OF DESIGN


To live a "hygge" lifestyle means to take time away from the rush of life to enjoy quieter pleasures, even if it's by yourself. Though this word comes from the Danish language, this idea was a way for many to find contentment during lonelier times.


This installation aims to romanticize hygge experiences like going home on a subway ride, the moments right before you go to sleep, or walking in a forest by yourself. Both a projection of a 2D animated forest, the same nature scene illustrated in VR, and accompanying sound design is used to evoke this feeling. Viewers are meant to enjoy the illustration and feel comforted.




Hygge was shown to a group of classmates. A limitation of VR is that only one person can fully view the experience, so while the classmates waited for their turn, a separate device was set up so that other students could see the same experience. A future iteration will have a website version as well.
